## Purpose of This Data Room
This data room is prepared to support institutional, strategic,
and healthcare-focused investors evaluating MeducateX at the
Discovery stage.

The materials are structured to provide clarity on the clinical
problem, the platform architecture, governance boundaries,
and the rationale for institutional adoption.

## Discovery vs. Deep Diligence
This repository primarily represents **Discovery-stage materials**.

Certain sections outline preparedness for Deep Diligence
(e.g., governance frameworks, compliance boundaries, and
advisory structures), but do not constitute final legal,
regulatory, or commercial documentation.

Deep Diligence materials are shared selectively and
progressively.

## Scope and Boundaries
MeducateX is an AI-enabled surgical education and risk-awareness
platform.

The platform is designed to support:
- Standardized surgical education
- Video-based learning and shadowing
- Risk and quality signal awareness
- Institutional governance and oversight

MeducateX does **not** provide medical advice, clinical decision
support, or autonomous diagnostic outputs.
