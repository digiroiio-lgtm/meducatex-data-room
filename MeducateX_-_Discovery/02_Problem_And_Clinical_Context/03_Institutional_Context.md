# 03 Institutional Context

**Purpose:** Provide the broader institutional backdrop (governance, safety priorities) that informs the problem statement.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

Hospitals and health systems are increasingly measured on safety, quality, and education metrics. There is growing demand for auditable training programs that link to credentialing, peer review, and governance committees. MeducateX fits into that context by offering a platform that documents learning and awareness without dictating care decisions.
