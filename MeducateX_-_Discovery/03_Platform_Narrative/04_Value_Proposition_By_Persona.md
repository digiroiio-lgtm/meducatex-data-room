# 04 Value Proposition By Persona

**Purpose:** Map persona-specific value with grounded language.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

Surgeons: Structured review without directive prompts, multi-angle content, and critical-moment callouts trigger active learning.  
Residents: Curated educational pathways, synchronized quizzes, and measurable certification to track progress.  
Hospital Admins: Governance-ready dashboards, compliance reporting, and audit trails to show training and risk visibility.  
Compliance Teams: RISQON risk layer outlines aggregate signals (anonymous, legal-safe) and connects them to institutional policy reviews.

These values describe observed use cases rather than promises.
