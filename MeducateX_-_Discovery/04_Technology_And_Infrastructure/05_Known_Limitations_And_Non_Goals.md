# 05 Known Limitations And Non Goals

**Purpose:** List current limitations and reinforce what MeducateX is intentionally not.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

Limitations include the need for human oversight, dependency on institutional governance review, and absence of autonomous clinical decision-making. Non-goals restate that MeducateX does not diagnose, predict, or prescribe, has no individual scoring, and keeps RISQON outputs aggregate-only.
