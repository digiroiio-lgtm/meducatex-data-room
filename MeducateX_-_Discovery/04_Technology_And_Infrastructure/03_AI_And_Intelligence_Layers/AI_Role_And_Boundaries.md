# AI Role And Boundaries

**Purpose:** Clarify how AI supports the platform and what it does not do.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

AI assists with content tagging, anonymization, best-angle selection, multilingual subtitles, and surfaced risk-relevant moments. It scores recordability, filters low-quality footage, and assembles a "main surgical video" for learning while keeping raw clinical signals inaccessible. All outputs remain advisory, human-reviewed, and intentionally non-diagnostic.
