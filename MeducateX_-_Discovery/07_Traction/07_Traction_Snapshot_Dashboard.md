# 07 Traction & Validation Snapshot

**Purpose:** Consolidate live traction indicators for VC diligence, highlighting what was deliberately tested, what moved, and what remains intentionally dark while remaining institution-first.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

**Status:** Live  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue, institution-first validation  
**Scope:** Clinical education & workflow (non-diagnostic)

## What Was Tested
- Live, structured surgical education delivery using non-diagnostic content.  
- Expert-led content creation and validation, anchored by 12 senior surgeons.  
- Platform readiness across web, mobile, and beta VR experiences.  
- Institution-first engagement with residents, surgeons, and senior faculty.  
- Narrative framing focused on standardization and risk awareness rather than EdTech volume.  
- Governance/scope boundaries vetted through senior advisory sessions.

## What Moved
- Content is live and delivered inside clinical education programs via structured courses.  
- Experts are active in content delivery, validation, and signature-surgery narratives.  
- Platform is production-ready (web + mobile live, VR layer validated for procedures).  
- Field validation executed at SIU Congress (Edinburgh) with targeted institutional meetings.  
- Senior advisory validation confirmed the Signature Surgery Flywheel logic, non-diagnostic education layer, and governance posture.  
- Early global relevance signals surfaced in LATAM, Europe, Africa, and MENA even as U.S. focus remains primary.  
- These movements reflect live execution traction, not revenue generation.
