# 02 Pilots

**Purpose:** Detail pilots and their scope without implying guaranteed follow-on business.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

- Pilot A (Dec 2025): Surgical education office at a Midwest academic hospital used MeducateX for three thoracic surgery teams, capturing debriefs and peer reflections.  
- Pilot B (Jan 2026): A national safety collaborative integrated MeducateX into their review cycle for laparoscopic procedures; the collaborative is evaluating next steps.

Each pilot is described with dates, partners, and measured outcomes (usage, completion) while noting that future expansions depend on institutional approval processes.
