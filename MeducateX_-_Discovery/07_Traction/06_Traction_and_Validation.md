**Status:** Live  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue, institution-first validation

## 1. Traction Summary (What Is Live Today)
MeducateX has moved from concept to live clinical execution with content, experts, and the platform actively deployed in surgical education settings. Core live signals: content is live, experts are active, the platform is production-ready, validation spans multiple countries, and pilots confirm the system functions inside live workflows.

- **Educational supply:** 24 structured surgical courses are live, designed for procedure-intensive specialties with a standardized format aligned to institutional training needs.  
- **Expert network:** 12 expert surgeons onboarded as lecturers and content leads, actively contributing to content delivery and validation, creating peer-led credibility signals.
- **Risk reduced:** content relevance and expert adoption risk.

## 3. Product & Platform Readiness
- **Platform status:** Web and mobile applications are live, with a beta VR layer ready for procedural education use cases.  
- **Operational proof:** Content delivery operates inside real training workflows, supporting recorded, structured, and hybrid education.  
- **Risk reduced:** product readiness and delivery/execution risk.

## 4. Field Validation — SIU Congress 2025 (Scotland)
- **Event:** SIU Congress, Edinburgh (Oct 29–Nov 1).  
- **What happened:** First field activation at a major international urology congress, transitioning from desk research to live clinical engagement.  
- **Engagement model:** Invitation-based 1:1 discussions with urology residents, surgeons, professors, and association members via booth and closed meetings.  
- **Outcome:** Early confirmation of surgical education gaps, validation of standardization needs, and clear signals on institutional fit/adoption boundaries.  
- **Risk reduced:** market reality and buyer relevance risk.

## 5. Senior Advisory & Strategic Validation
- **Post-congress advisory (Istanbul):** Strategic sessions with Prof. Jean de la Rosette (SIU President) and Prof. Laguna.  
- **Focus:** Scope boundaries, institutional positioning, platform credibility, and long-term pathways.  
- **Outcome:** Validation of the Signature Surgery Flywheel, endorsement of a non-diagnostic education-linked risk layer, and identification of new institutional growth pathways.  
- **Risk reduced:** strategic direction and governance/scope risk.
