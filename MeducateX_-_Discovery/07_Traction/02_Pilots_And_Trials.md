# 02 Pilots And Trials

**Purpose:** Capture the scope and outcomes of pilots in descriptive terms.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

Pilot A (Dec 2025): Captured 3 thoracic surgery teams at a Midwest academic hospital with governance-aligned debriefs.  
Pilot B (Jan 2026): Safety collaborative integrated MeducateX into laparoscopic reviews.  
Additionally, MeducateX pilots include field execution support (MeduShip) with embedded educators, highlighting how capture, review, and governance operate together. Outcomes are described without implying guaranteed expansion.
