# 01 Roadmap Overview

**Purpose:** Present the directional roadmap with clarity about timing and dependencies.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

The roadmap covers three high-level buckets: platform governance features, analytics maturity, and institutional enablement. Each bucket lists planned work for the next 12 months with dependencies on clinical council reviews and partner availability. Dates are directional (Q2 2026, Q4 2026) and labeled as contingent on approval and capacity.
