# 02 Pricing Logic

**Purpose:** Explain how pricing is structured and how assumptions were derived.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

Pricing consists of a base institutional license plus optional modules for governance reporting. Seat-based tiers reflect hospital size and number of operating theaters. Pricing logic is tied to service levels and administrative overhead rather than inflated TAM multiples. All assumptions are recorded to assist diligence reviewers in understanding the rationale.
