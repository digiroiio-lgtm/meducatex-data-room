# 01 Go-To-Market Strategy

**Purpose:** Describe the execution approach for reaching the ICPs without speculative guarantees.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-21

The GTM motion targets three tracks: direct pursuit of hospitals with dedicated safety programs, partnerships with academic societies, and referrals from advisory physicians. Engagement milestones include establishing steering committees, documenting pilot objectives, and aligning on measurement frameworks. Assumptions are limited to existing institutional readiness signals and the capacity of the internal team to pursue the named segments.
